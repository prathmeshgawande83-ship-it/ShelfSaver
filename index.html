Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping safely on ShelfSaver.

Are the products expired? ▾
No, ShelfSaver only sells products before their expiry date. Every product listing clearly shows the exact expiry date and the time remaining, in weeks or months. We never list or allow expired products on the platform.
Why are prices lower? ▾
Products are discounted because they have a shorter shelf life remaining — not because of any quality issue. Sellers discount these to move inventory before expiry, and that saving is passed directly to you.
Can I see expiry dates before buying? ▾
Yes, every product listing on ShelfSaver clearly displays the exact expiry date and the time remaining, shown in weeks or months. We believe in full transparency so you can always make an informed purchase decision.
What is the refund policy? ▾
Customers can request returns if products don't match the listing details. Full refunds are processed within 24 hours of approval. We have a zero-hassle return policy to ensure complete satisfaction.
How do I become a seller? ▾
Register as a seller, upload your business documents and FSSAI registration, and our team will verify your account within 48 hours. Once approved, you can start listing your near-expiry products immediately.
Is my payment information safe? ▾
All payments are processed through Razorpay, India's leading payment gateway with bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers.
